End of days!

@karindebruin

Description

Last week I had to catch a flight to Malaga but there was something in the sky that made me go to the water first. Sometimes a beautiful day ends with a beautiful sunset. But this night a dull, cold and rainy day ended with the strangest sky ever. The dark clouds came rolling in but the sun managed to shine some light at the horizon.
